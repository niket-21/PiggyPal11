Getting Started with PiggyPal

What is PiggyPal?

PiggyPal is a pocket money management tool designed to help you track expenses, create budgets, set savings goals, and learn money-saving tips. It's a completely frontend application that runs in your browser with no server requirements.

How do I get started with PiggyPal?

To get started with PiggyPal:

  1. Navigate to the Home page to see your financial dashboard
  2. Set up your monthly budget on the Budget page
  3. Start tracking your expenses on the Expenses page
  4. Create savings goals on the Goals page

All your data is stored locally on your device, so you don't need to create an account.

Is my data secure with PiggyPal?

Yes! PiggyPal stores all your financial data locally on your device using browser local storage. Your data never leaves your device or gets sent to any server. This means:

  • Your financial information stays private
  • No internet connection is required after the initial page load
  • No account creation or login needed

Note: Clearing your browser data will erase your PiggyPal information. Consider backing up your data regularly using the export feature.

Can I use PiggyPal on my mobile device?

Yes! PiggyPal is designed with a responsive layout that works on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. The interface will automatically adjust to fit your screen size for the best experience.

Tracking Expenses

How do I add a new expense?

To add a new expense:

  1. Go to the Expenses page
  2. Fill out the "Add New Expense" form with details like title, amount, date, and category
  3. Click the "Add Expense" button

Your expense will be saved and will appear in your expense history and dashboard summaries.

How can I edit or delete an expense?

To edit or delete an existing expense:

  1. Go to the Expenses page
  2. Find the expense in your expense history table
  3. Click the edit (pencil) icon to modify the expense
  4. In the edit modal, make your changes and click "Save Changes"
  5. To delete, click the "Delete" button in the edit modal

Can I filter or search my expenses?

Yes! On the Expenses page, you can:

  • Use the search box to find expenses by title or notes
  • Click the filter button to open advanced filtering options
  • Filter by date range, categories, or amount range
  • Sort your expenses by clicking on the table headers

How can I see expense statistics and charts?

PiggyPal provides several ways to visualize your expenses:

  • On the Expenses page, you'll find the Expense Breakdown chart showing category distributions
  • Toggle between Week, Month, and Year views to see different time periods
  • The Home page dashboard shows a summary pie chart of your expenses
  • The summary cards at the top of the Expenses page show quick statistics for today, this week, and this month

Budget Planning

How do I set up my budget?

To set up your budget:

  1. Go to the Budget page
  2. Click the "Set Budget" button
  3. Select the month and enter your total budget amount
  4. Click "Set Budget" to save
  5. Use the "Adjust Categories" button to allocate your budget across different spending categories

What is the Budget Calculator?

The Budget Calculator is a tool that helps you determine how to allocate your pocket money across different spending categories. It offers two methods:

  • 50/30/20 Rule: Automatically divides your money with 50% for needs, 30% for wants, and 20% for savings
  • Custom Allocation: Lets you set your own percentage for each category based on your priorities

To use it, click the "Open Calculator" button in the Budget Tools section of the Budget page.

How does PiggyPal track my budget progress?

PiggyPal automatically tracks your budget progress by:

  • Comparing your expenses to your budget limits
  • Showing progress bars for overall budget and individual categories
  • Calculating remaining amounts and percentages
  • Displaying visual charts of your budget allocation and usage

As you add expenses, your budget progress updates automatically.

Can I adjust my budget mid-month?

Yes! You can adjust your budget at any time:

  • To change the total budget amount, click "Set Budget" and enter a new amount
  • To reallocate money between categories, click "Adjust Categories"
  • Your budget progress will automatically recalculate based on your changes

This flexibility allows you to adapt your budget as your needs change throughout the month.

Savings Goals

How do I create a savings goal?

To create a new savings goal:

  1. Go to the Goals page
  2. Fill out the "Create New Goal" form with a title, target amount, target date, and category
  3. Optionally add an initial deposit and notes
  4. Click "Create Goal"

Your goal will appear in the goals tracker section below.

How do I track progress toward my goals?

PiggyPal provides several ways to track your goal progress:

  • The Goals page shows visual progress bars for each of your goals
  • Click on any goal to see detailed information including:
    • A circular progress indicator showing percentage complete
    • Amount saved, target amount, and remaining amount
    • Time remaining until your target date
    • Deposit history
  • The Home page dashboard shows your active goals and their progress

How do I add money to my savings goal?

To add a deposit to your savings goal:

  1. Go to the Goals page
  2. Click on the goal you want to update
  3. In the goal details modal, click "Add Deposit"
  4. Enter the deposit amount, date, and optional notes
  5. Click "Add Deposit" to save

Your goal progress will automatically update to reflect the new deposit.

What is the Savings Goal Calculator?

The Savings Goal Calculator helps you determine how much you need to save regularly to reach your financial goals. To use it:

  1. Enter your goal amount
  2. Specify your timeframe in weeks or months
  3. Optionally enter an initial amount you've already saved
  4. Click "Calculate"

The calculator will show you how much you need to save weekly and monthly to reach your goal by the target date.

Money-Saving Tips

How do I find tips relevant to my situation?

To find relevant money-saving tips:

  • Visit the Saving Tips page
  • Use the filter buttons to view tips by category (Daily Habits, Shopping, Planning, Earning)
  • Use the search box to find tips by keyword
  • Browse through the tips grid to discover new ideas
  • Bookmark your favorite tips for easy reference later

How do I bookmark tips for later?

To bookmark a money-saving tip:

  1. Find a tip you want to save on the Saving Tips page
  2. Click the bookmark icon (🔖) on the tip card
  3. The tip will be saved to your "Bookmarked Tips" section at the bottom of the page
  4. You can access your bookmarked tips anytime you visit the Saving Tips page

What is the Monthly Saving Challenge?

The Monthly Saving Challenge is a fun way to build better saving habits. Each month features a different challenge to help you save money. To participate:

  1. Visit the Saving Tips page to see the current month's challenge
  2. Click "Join Challenge" to participate
  3. Follow the challenge guidelines throughout the month
  4. Track your progress and see how the community is doing

Challenges are designed to be achievable while helping you develop money-saving habits.

Can I share my own money-saving tips?

Yes! PiggyPal encourages users to share their own money-saving strategies. To submit a tip:

  1. Scroll down to the "Share Your Saving Tip" section on the Saving Tips page
  2. Fill out the form with your tip title, description, and category
  3. Click "Submit Tip"

Your tip will be saved locally and will appear in your tips collection. In this static frontend version, tips are only visible to you on your device.

Data & Privacy

Where is my data stored?

PiggyPal stores all your data locally on your device using browser local storage. This means:

  • Your financial information never leaves your device
  • No data is sent to any servers or cloud storage
  • Your information is private and only accessible on your current device
  • No internet connection is required after the initial page load

How can I back up my PiggyPal data?

To back up your PiggyPal data:

  1. Go to the Settings section (accessible from the user menu in the top right)
  2. Click on "Export Data"
  3. A JSON file containing all your PiggyPal data will be downloaded
  4. Store this file securely on your computer or cloud storage

We recommend backing up your data regularly to prevent loss if your browser data is cleared.

How do I restore my data from a backup?

To restore your data from a backup:

  1. Go to the Settings section
  2. Click on "Import Data"
  3. Select your backup JSON file
  4. Confirm that you want to replace your current data
  5. Your data will be restored from the backup file

Note: Importing data will overwrite any existing data in your PiggyPal application.

How do I delete all my data from PiggyPal?

To delete all your data from PiggyPal:

  1. Go to the Settings section
  2. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section
  3. Click on "Reset All Data"
  4. Confirm that you want to permanently delete all your data

Alternatively, you can clear your browser's local storage through your browser settings, which will also remove all PiggyPal data.

Troubleshooting

My data disappeared after closing the browser. What happened?

If your data disappeared, it might be due to one of these reasons:

  • You cleared your browser data/cache, which erased the local storage
  • You're using private/incognito browsing mode, which doesn't persist local storage
  • You're using a different browser or device than before
  • Your browser has limited local storage or is configured to clear it on exit

To prevent data loss in the future, regularly export your data as a backup using the Settings > Export Data option.

The charts and graphs aren't displaying correctly. How can I fix this?

If charts and graphs aren't displaying correctly:

  1. Make sure you're using an up-to-date browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  2. Try refreshing the page
  3. Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings
  4. Try clearing your browser cache and reloading the page
  5. If using a mobile device, ensure you're not in data-saving mode

If problems persist, try accessing PiggyPal from a different browser or device.

The app is running slowly. How can I improve performance?

If PiggyPal is running slowly, try these solutions:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Close other tabs and applications to free up memory
  • If you have a large amount of data (hundreds of expenses or transactions), consider exporting and backing up your data, then resetting the app
  • Check if your browser needs updating
  • Try using a different browser

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Since PiggyPal is a static frontend project, there's no direct bug reporting system. However, you can:

  • Check if the issue is related to your browser by trying a different one
  • Clear your browser cache and local storage to see if that resolves the issue
  • If you're a developer, you can inspect the browser console for any error messages
  • As this is a demonstration project, bugs can be reported to the project creator if contact information is provided
