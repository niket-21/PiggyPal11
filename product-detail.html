Product Description

Introducing our Premium Wireless Earbuds, the perfect companion for music lovers and professionals alike. These earbuds combine cutting-edge technology with sleek design to deliver an exceptional audio experience.

The active noise cancellation technology blocks out ambient noise, allowing you to focus on your music or calls without distractions. With Bluetooth 5.2, you get a stable connection up to 10 meters away from your device.

The ergonomic design ensures a comfortable fit for extended wear, while the touch controls make it easy to manage your music, calls, and voice assistant. The earbuds are IPX7 water-resistant, making them perfect for workouts and outdoor activities.

With up to 8 hours of playback time on a single charge and an additional 22 hours with the charging case, you can enjoy your music all day long. The quick charge feature gives you 2 hours of playback with just 10 minutes of charging.
